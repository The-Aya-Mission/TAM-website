Program Overview

The Aya Mission program helps Veterans, their families, caregivers, and survivors. We offer peer preparation coaching, integration support, community connection, and access to mental health resources for veterans pursuing alternative healing.

How the Program Works

1 · Prepare

Peer preparation coaching and evidence-based education about benefits, risks, and how to get ready — mind, body, and spirit.

2 · Ceremony

Sacramental ceremonies are conducted by independent religious organizations — trusted partners with their own enrollment process. TAM provides support before and after.

3 · Integrate

Structured integration support and a Peer Support Group where healing continues long after ceremony ends.

Apply for Preparation & Integration Support

Veterans who are accepted into our program receive a direct link to our partner church's independent ceremony registration — the church conducts its own separate enrollment process. Complete TAM's support intake form below to begin.

Questions before you apply?

Explore our mental health resources or reach out through any of our channels.
