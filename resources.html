Non-VA Mental Health Support

Seeking support is vital for mental well-being, and there are many excellent non-VA mental health resources available to provide compassionate care tailored to individual needs.

Sarai Darbandi, LCSW

Begin your transformative healing journey with a compassionate psychedelic integration therapist who creates a safe, judgment-free space for profound personal growth — a unique blend of evidence-based approaches to help you process insights, navigate challenges, and cultivate lasting positive change.

Integration Support by Sheryl

Begin your journey toward natural healing and resilience with Organic Intelligence® Coaching. Your body has an innate capacity to process stress and trauma when given the right support and guidance — moving from feeling overwhelmed to greater presence and enjoyment in life.

Community Peer Support Groups We Trust

Veteran-led peer support groups create community by connecting individuals with shared experiences, fostering understanding and trust. These groups empower veterans to support one another, promoting healing through shared stories and camaraderie.

Apricus Coaching

Whether you are seeking a plan, accountability, help with a specific issue, or personal empowerment — clarify your vision of the future and build an actionable path forward, based on a deeper understanding of your truest self.

Valorz Veteran Community AZ

Connecting Veterans to their community and veteran resources — building camaraderie through local community events, volunteer activities, and veteran resource events for employment, education, and more.

Arizona Trail Vets Program

Connects Veterans and peers with opportunities to get involved with a community of advocacy with nature. Veterans come together to improve our state and national parks.

Merging Vets and Players (MVP)

From the battlefield to the playing field — helping veterans and athletes redirect their strength, excellence, and dedication into a new mission that provides meaning and purpose.
