Get Involved

The Aya Mission runs on people who show up — veterans and civilians alike. Whether you have an hour a month or a skill to share, there's a place for you here.

✺

Volunteer

Serve as an intake coordinator, peer support specialist, integration coach, social media coordinator, or runner ambassador. Training and clear role agreements provided for every position.

✺

Partner With Us

Veteran organizations, clinicians, and community groups: collaborate on referrals, co-hosted events, and awareness campaigns that expand what's possible for veteran healing.

✺

Sponsor & Affiliate

Corporate sponsors and brand ambassadors help fund ceremonies, meals, coaching, and research. Prospective affiliates go through a straightforward onboarding process.

Reach Out

Tell us how you'd like to serve. Email us and a member of our team will follow up with next steps, role details, and onboarding.

Email Contact@the.ayamission.org

Prefer to start smaller? Join the newsletter or grab some merch — every bit helps.

Crisis Support
