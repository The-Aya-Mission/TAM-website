About Entheogenic Healing

Honest education — benefits, risks, and why preparation and integration matter most.

What Are Entheogens?

Entheogens are plant-based substances — like ayahuasca — used for generations in sacred and ceremonial contexts by cultures around the world. In recent years, a growing body of clinical research into psychedelic-assisted approaches has renewed scientific interest in their potential to help people process trauma, depression, anxiety, and addiction when used in supported, intentional settings.

They are not a magic cure, and they are not right for everyone. Certain medical conditions, psychiatric histories, and medications can make participation unsafe — which is why honest screening and education come before anything else.

Why Preparation & Integration Matter
✺

Prepare

Education, intention-setting, and readiness — mind, body, and spirit. Veterans in our community consistently say prior therapy made their experience more meaningful.

✺

Experience

Sacramental ceremonies are conducted by independent religious organizations with their own enrollment processes. TAM's role is support — before and after.

✺

Integrate

The ceremony is a beginning, not an ending. Structured integration and peer support are where insights become lasting change in daily life.

What We Measure

We don't ask anyone to take healing on faith. Veterans in our program complete PHQ-9 and GAD-7 clinical assessments before ceremony and at follow-up, and our research program is developing EEG-based studies under an Institutional Review Board. See the outcomes so far on our Impact & Research page.

This page is education, not medical advice. Every veteran's situation is different — talk with your healthcare providers, and be fully transparent during our intake process.
